Assignment Brief

The Brief

Choose a small enterprise, neighborhood shop, or roadside store for an in-depth rebranding initiative. Talk with the owner to learn about the brand's background, principles, and range of products. Identify a distinctive feature that characterises the business and include it in the redesign. The aim is to develop a renewed brand identity that improves its visual attractiveness while preserving its authenticity and bond with its customers.

About the Brand

K T Garib Bakery

K T Garib Bakery in Bhagat Ki Kothi, Jodhpur is a renowned bakery offering a delectable range of freshly baked goods and pastries. Situated in a prime location in Jodhpur, the bakery attracts food lovers from all over the area. With an impressive 4.2 rating based on reviews, K T Garib Bakery is a go-to destination for those looking to indulge in delightful baked treats — offering everything from bread and cakes to cookies and pastries.

Brand Strategy

Mission, Vision & Values

Mission

Delivering exceptional baked treats crafted with care, precision, and the finest ingredients. Upholding values of affordability, community, and authenticity — spreading happiness, one bite at a time.

Vision

To become a beloved neighbourhood bakery that symbolises warmth, quality, and tradition, creating a lasting bond with customers through freshly baked goods and timeless flavours.

Values & Positioning

Quality, affordability, and authenticity. Rooted in tradition, fostering community while embracing modern flavours — a trusted, community-focused bakery at affordable prices in Jodhpur.

Logo System

Primary & Secondary Mark

Primary Logo

The Turban Mark

The logo prominently features a vibrant red turban — a hallmark of Rajasthani culture — celebrating the bakery's roots in Jodhpur. A classic black moustache adds charm and cultural authenticity, while a golden circular frame signifies timelessness and trust. "Est. 1948" honours the bakery's long-standing heritage.

Secondary Logo

The Bilingual Mark

Combining minimalism with cultural significance, this mark features stylised Devanagari "ग" and "B" — symbolising bilingual identity and broad appeal. A warm red-to-yellow gradient reflects Rajasthani vibrancy, the iconic moustache adding regional personality.
