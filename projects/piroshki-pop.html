Design Brief

The Brief

This project focused on building a fresh and playful identity for a company that makes and distributes traditional Russian pastries. The goal was to appeal to a millennial audience by blending nostalgia with a modern, fun aesthetic. The brief included the creation of a unique and memorable brand name along with a complete branding package—visual identity, logo design, packaging, and digital touchpoints. Inspired by diner culture and retro vibes, the design had to feel warm and bold. The brand needed to be approachable, rooted in tradition, yet visually distinct in the competitive food and beverage space.

About the Brand

Brand Identity

Piroshki Pop! comes alive in vibrant, nostalgic settings that speak directly to millennials. Whether it's a cozy diner corner, a fun food truck, or a cheerful bakery counter, the brand shines through bold colors, playful typography, and interactive packaging. The creamy reds and soft beige tones feel familiar yet fresh, creating a warm and energetic vibe. Custom props, branded takeaway boxes, and social-ready backdrops invite customers to engage beyond just the product. Every touchpoint — from signage to merch — celebrates the fusion of tradition and fun, making Piroshki Pop! more than a snack; it's a full experience.

Mission & Vision

Purpose

Mission

Piroshki Pop! aims to reimagine traditional Russian pastries with fresh ingredients and playful energy. We bring joy to every bite, serving millennial audiences with nostalgic flavors wrapped in modern branding. Our mission is to create fun, memorable food moments that are as delicious as they are Instagrammable.

Vision

To become a beloved comfort food brand that brings generations together. Piroshki Pop! envisions a future where Russian pastries are a staple in diners, pop-ups, and homes across the globe—celebrated for their warmth, personality, and twist of fun that turns everyday snacking into joyful experiences.

Brand Name

Why Piroshki Pop?

The name "Piroshki Pop!" perfectly captures the essence of the brand. Piroshki directly references the traditional Russian pastry, grounding the brand in cultural authenticity. The word Pop! adds a burst of fun, energy, and modern appeal—qualities that resonate strongly with the millennial audience. It also evokes a retro diner feel, aligning with the brand's nostalgic yet playful direction. The name is catchy, memorable, and visually expressive, making it ideal for building a vibrant, Instagrammable identity.

Logo

Primary Logo

The primary logo showcases a slice of cake at the center, surrounded by the name "Piroshki Pop!" in a bold, bubbly typeface within a vibrant red circular badge. The rounded, playful lettering and soft pastel illustration reflect the brand's fun and nostalgic energy.

Secondary Logo

The secondary logo presents a bold, stacked letterform arrangement of "Piroshki," with an exaggerated, playful "P" leading the design. Set within a red circular badge, this compact version retains the brand's fun, nostalgic character while ensuring flexibility across smaller applications.
