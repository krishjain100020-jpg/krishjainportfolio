STORY.

A bespoke menswear brand — crafted for couples who wear their narrative.

Overview

Where tailoring meets
emotion

STORY is a bespoke menswear brand selling exclusively online, targeting couples who appreciate customized, luxurious fashion. The goal was to create a white pictorial logo that reflects both glamour and professionalism, while being suitable for embroidery.

The project deliverables included a logo concept, tagline integration, and contextual mockups.

Design Concept

Symbol & meaning

The logo combines a diamond shape with a needle, forming a single mark that carries multiple layers of intention.

◇

Diamond Form

The diamond silhouette communicates luxury and enduring value — fitting for a brand built around milestone occasions and lasting style.

⌇

The Needle

A fine needle runs through the mark, signalling precision tailoring and the craft behind every garment. Minimal lines, maximum intention.

◎

Symmetry

The symmetrical composition reflects unity — two people, one story. The white palette adds touch of luxury and reads perfectly when embroidered.

Primary Logo

The pictorial mark

The primary logomark stands alone — a geometric emblem that works at scale, in thread, and in digital contexts equally well.

Dark background

Secondary Logo

The wordmark

This clean wordmark version emphasizes the brand name "STORY" in a bold, elegant serif font. It retains a luxurious and professional look, making it perfect for embroidery, packaging, and digital use where space is limited.

Simple yet impactful, it keeps the brand identity consistent while offering flexibility across touchpoints.

Mockups & Applications

Logo in context

Visualising the mark across real touchpoints — cards, packaging, and embroidery — to validate how the identity holds in the hands of its audience.
