About the Brand

JACK &
JONES

Jack & Jones is a Danish fashion brand established in 1990, under the Bestseller Group. It specializes in high-quality men's clothing, including casual wear, denim, and tailored apparel.

Known for its trendy yet affordable styles, Jack & Jones caters to young, fashion-conscious men. The brand emphasizes craftsmanship and innovative design, particularly in denim, which remains a cornerstone of its identity. Its global presence spans standalone stores and shop-in-shop formats.

Concept Note

BROTHERHOOD
VIBES

This campaign celebrates the joy of camaraderie and shared experiences in casual, energetic settings — sports fields, BBQs, and hangouts. Dynamic visuals capture friends playing, laughing, or jamming together, prioritising genuine connections over posed shots.

Props like skateboards and guitars enhance the vibe throughout, reinforcing the sense of a crew that lives in the moment.
